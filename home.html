What We Detect

Eczema

A chronic condition causing red, itchy, and inflamed skin. Often triggered by allergens or stress.

Melanoma

A serious form of skin cancer. Early detection is key to effective treatment.

Atopic Dermatitis

A type of eczema often seen in children. Causes dry, itchy patches on the skin.

Basal Cell Carcinoma

A slow-growing type of skin cancer that appears as a waxy bump or scar-like lesion.

Melanotic Nevi

Commonly known as moles. Some moles may change and require attention to rule out melanoma.
